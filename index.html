Corporate Couture: The High-heeled Hustle

Introduction

Corporate Couture: The High-heeled Hustle is a strategic board game that simulates the challenges and opportunities of entering the corporate world in the fashion industry. Players navigate through various scenarios reflecting real-world dynamics in corporate settings.

Objective

The objective of the game is to be the first to land on the "Job Offer" space, using strategic decisions and opportunities to advance.

Game Setup

Each player chooses a character and sets up the board with opportunity and activity cards shuffled and placed on designated spots.

Gameplay

Players take turns spinning the wheel to advance on the board, facing various work-related scenarios through activity and opportunity cards that could propel or hinder their progress.

Characters

From the fashion-savvy Nepotism Baby to the hardworking Business Gal, each character comes with unique backgrounds and challenges.

Winning the Game

The game is won by the first player who lands exactly on the "Job Offer" space, successfully navigating the pitfalls and opportunities of the corporate world.

Moral of the Game

This game is designed to reflect the inequalities and varied chances in the corporate world, sparking discussions on necessary changes within the industry.
