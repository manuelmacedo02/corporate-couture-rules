Corporate Couture: The High-heeled Hustle

Premise of the Game

Young interns enter the workplace for the first time, encountering problems, challenges, opportunities, benefits, and navigating the circumstances they are put in.

The game can be adapted to suit any of the end goals (e.g., celebrity stylist's assistant).

Objective

The objective of Corporate Couture is to be the first player to reach the "Job Offer" space at the end of the board, symbolizing successful job attainment, based on a degree of luck, but also using your knowledge to take advantage of opportunity card boosters.

Setup

Each player draws a character figurine, each with pre-set personalities and life situations that may dictate how their wheel moves count.

Set up the game board, which is laid out with spaces leading from "Start" to "Job Offer."

Shuffle and place the Activity Cards and Opportunity Cards in designated piles on the board.

Game Play

Players take turns spinning the wheel to move forward on the board. The number may multiply depending on who the character is.

If a player lands on an Activity Space, they draw an Activity Card which could either advance or set them back based on realistic work-related scenarios; these cards can affect other players' game status as well.

Landing on an Opportunity Space allows players to answer the question written on it. This question is read by another player. Answering correctly lets the player move up two additional spaces.

Winning the Game

The first player to land on the "Job Offer" space wins the game.
